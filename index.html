FOOD & BREWERIES

Reccomendations from Local Richmonders!

Burger Bach

3426 W Cary St, Richmond, VA 23221

Grass-fed beef underlines this New Zealander gastropub decked in plank woods & corrugated tin. All of the burgers are great, but you have to try the fries with a bunch of their different dipping options!

Must try dish: The Hangover Cure Burger

Cobra Burger

400 N 27th St, Richmond, VA 23223

A very new burger restaurant that makes a unique smash-style burger that makes your mouth water.

Must try dish: The Cobra Burger

Carytown Burgers & Fries

3449 W Cary St Richmond, VA 23221

Beef & veggie burgers with many toppings served counter style in a laid-back space with patio.

Must try dish: Rodeo Burger

PBR

2553 W Cary St, Richmond, VA 23220

They serve neapolitan style pizzas that are cooked in an authentic Marri Forni brick oven.
(PRO TIP: you can get any of their pizzas ½ and ½ )

Must try dish: Fight Club Pizza

Zorch

2923 W Cary St, Richmond, VA 23221

Friendly neighborhood New York style pizza joint with a roaming food truck.

Must try dish: The Upside Down Pizza

Triple Crossing- Fulton

5203 Hatcher St, Richmond, VA 23231

Spacious, beautiful spot with outdoor and indoor areas with a friendly vibe.

Must try dish: Hot honey pepperoni pizza (pair every meal with a Falcon Smash)

Edos Squid

411 N Harrison St, Richmond, VA 23220

Casual brick-lined Italian restaurant with a full bar & plenty of calamari dishes on the menu.

Must try dish: Squid Genovese

Gersi

805 N Davis Ave, Richmond, VA 23220

Very cozy restaurant with NYC roots. They have a beautiful outdoor area.

Must try dish: Orecchiette with Broccoli Rabe & Italian Sausage.

Dinamo

821 W Cary St, Richmond, VA 23220

Petite Italian restaurant dishing up pizzas, pasta & seafood dishes in a convivial ambiance.

Must try dish: Their menu changes very frequently, ask for your servers recommendations!

Lunch and Supper

1215 Summit Ave Richmond, VA

A Classic Southern Smokehouse tucked away in RVA's latest up and coming neighborhood, surrounded by local breweries, local produce, and local farms. LUNCH.SUPPER! utilizes all that Richmond has to offer to bring you our version of a homegrown experience.

Must try dish: Pickled and Fried

Little Nickel

4702 Forest Hill Ave, Richmond, VA 23225

Little Nickel is a neighborhood restaurant. The cuisine could best be described as "tropical diner"- a splash of coastal Mediterranean flair & spirited twists on mid-century American classics alongside thoughtful & funky cocktails.

Must try dish: Hawaiian Pork/chicken Bowl

ZZQ

3201 W Moore St Richmond, VA 23230

Named one of the top 40 best BBQ joints in America. We think it should be #1! The ZZCrew works 24 hours a day to make delicious BBQ. They are open until they sell out, which they usually do by mid-afternoon.

Must try dish: One of everything. Bring friends to share with!

Alewife

3120 E Marshall St, Richmond, VA 23223

Bright, acclaimed eatery offering sophisticated dishes based on Mid-Atlantic seafood & cocktails.

Must try dish: daily special

Brenner Pass

3200 Rockbridge St #100, Richmond, VA 23230

Stylish, modern destination for chef-driven European dining & desserts with craft cocktails & wines.

Must try dish: Robiola Agnolotti

Stella's

1012 Lafayette St, Richmond, VA 23221

Modern Greek fare, including small plates, in a blond-wood minimalist space with a communal table.

Must try dish: Pastichio

Mama J's

415 N 1st St., Richmond, VA 23219

Family owned, black owned restaurant located in historic Jackson Ward.

Must Try: Catfish dinner

Big Herm's

315 N 2nd St, Richmond, VA 23219

Black-owned southern take-out spot.

Must Try Dish: Southern Fried Wings

Lillie Pearl

416 E Grace St., Richmond, VA 23219

Black-owned restaurant serving you impeccable southern dishes and giving you great hospitality experience.

Must Try Dish: Fried Chicken Sandwich Combo

Peter Chang's

2816 W Broad St A, Richmond, VA 23230

Award winning chef Peter Chang brings traditional Szechuan cuisine to the heart of RVA. With family style dishes and great appetizers, there is something for everyone to enjoy.

Must Try Dish: Pan fried pork dumplings ad dried fried eggplant

My Noodle & Bar

1600 Monument Ave, Richmond, VA 23220

Thai restaurant in bar established in 2013 serving you quality meals and delicacies.

Must Try Dish: Drunken Noodles

Sabai

2727 W Broad St, Richmond, VA 23220

Thai restaurant which incorprates design from Artist, Brandon Pearson. Sabai serves you authentic dishes and delicacies from the streets of Bangkok.

Must Try Dish: Pad Thai

Y Tu Mama

4910 Forest Hill Ave, Richmond, VA 23225

This Mexican food spot is inside of The Veil Brewing company. They have great food to go along with delicious Veil beer.

Must Try Dish: Quesobarria Tacos

TBT El Gallo

2118 W Cary St, Richmond, VA 23220

Quirky counter-serve dishing up creative Mexican street food in a compact space.

Must Try Dish: Birria tacos

Soul Taco

321 N 2nd St, Richmond, VA 23219

Black owned taco restaurant located in RVA andd soon to be Raleigh, NC, bringing you a mix of Latin, American, and Southern flavors.

Must Try Dish: Buttermilk Battered Fried Chicken Taco

Joe's Inn

205 N Shields Ave, Richmond, VA 23220

Retro eatery opened in 1952 known for its broad comfort-food menu, vintage vibe & adjacent bar.

Must Try Dish: Spaghetti A La Greek

Village Cafe

1001 W Grace St, Richmond, VA 23220

Funky local institution since 1956 for eclectic comfort fare & an offbeat mix of characters.

Must Try Dish: Fried Provolone Sandwich

3 Monkeys

2524 W Main St, Richmond, VA 23220

Greek fare plus sandwiches & pizza are offered in a stylish tavern space with a bar & patio.

Must Try Dish: Shrimp & Chorizo Po Boy3

Eat my Eggroll

Check link for address!

Egg Roll Shake Shop. Big Dope Egg Rolls & other things you can bite. Ultimate Munchie Truck.

Must Try Dish: Avocado fries

Taqueria Panchito

Visit Facebook for current location or visit their brick and mortar at 6531 Midlothian Tpke, Richmond, VA 23225

No fuss, authentic Mexican food.

Must Try Dish: Al pastor tacos

Intergalactic Tacos

Instagram

Solid Mexican fusion food with classic and inventive food, updated regularly.

Must Try Dish: Get a space box to try multiple tacos and their sauces. Don’t forget to add a side of queso!
